About Me

Hi there! I’m a recent graduate in International Relations from Jahangirnagar University. Beyond my academic pursuits, I’m also a passionate graphic designer and independent freelancer with over four years of experience. Graphic design has been my creative outlet, allowing me to work on diverse projects like logos, flyers, posters, and brochures.

Using tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, and Microsoft Office Suite, I’ve delivered designs that meet and exceed client expectations.
